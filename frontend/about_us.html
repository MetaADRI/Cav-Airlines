About Us

Soaring above expectations, rooted in tradition, and connected through a shared love for exceptional journeys.

Who we are

CAVAIR is Zambia's flagship airline with a global reach. Headquartered in Lusaka, we proudly connect Zambia to the world and the world to Zambia — offering seamless travel experiences across Africa, Europe, Asia, and beyond. As a symbol of national pride, we combine world-class aviation standards with the authentic warmth of Zambian hospitality.

Our Story

Established to transform the aviation landscape in Zambia, CAVAIR was founded with a bold vision—to bridge distances, create opportunities, and elevate the standard of travel for Zambians and visitors alike. From humble beginnings as a domestic carrier, we’ve grown into an international airline trusted by travelers across borders.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver world-class, safe, and customer-focused air travel that connects Zambia to the global stage while redefining excellence in African aviation. At CAVAIR, we are committed to creating seamless travel experiences that reflect the highest international standards — from the ground to the skies.

Our drive has always propelled us past the ordinary, allowing us to create truly exceptional travel experiences.

What sets us apart?

Modern Fleet

Our state-of-the-art aircraft are maintained to the highest international safety standards for a comfortable, reliable journey.

Global Reach

We operate an expanding network of international routes, linking Zambia to the world. CAVAIR serves multiple continents.

Competitive Value

From economy to business class, enjoy the perfect combination of comfort and service at competitive global fares.
