About Us


Soaring above expectations, rooted in tradition, and connected through a shared love for exceptional journeys.







Who we are


CAVAIR is Zambia's flagship airline with a global reach. Headquartered in
Lusaka, we proudly connect Zambia to the world and the world to Zambia-
offering seamless travel experiences across Africa, Europe, Asia, and beyond.
As a symbol of national pride, we combine world-class aviation standards with
the authentic warmth of Zambian hospitality.




Our Story


Established to transform the aviation landscape in Zambia,
CAVAIR was founded with a bold vision-to bridge distances,
create opportunities, and elevate the standard of travel for
Zambians and visitors alike. From humble beginnings as a
domestic carrier, we've grown into an international airline
trusted by travellers across boarders.




Our Mission


Our mission is to deliver world-class, safe, and customer-focused
air travel that connects Zambia to the global stage while redefining
excellence in African aviation. At CAVAIR, we are committed to creating
seamless travel experiences that reflect the highest
international standards-from the ground to the skies.
